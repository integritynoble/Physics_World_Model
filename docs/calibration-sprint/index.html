What Is a Calibration Sprint?

A Calibration Sprint is a focused engagement where our team takes your imaging system's measurement data, calibrates the physics forward model, and delivers tuned operator parameters that maximize reconstruction quality.

You receive a complete RunBundle (v0.3.0) with calibrated parameters, reconstruction results, quality metrics, and actionable recommendations for your specific hardware and application.

What's Included

Operator Calibration

Fit forward-model parameters (dispersion, PSF, gain, shift, mask alignment) to your measured data using PWM's UPWMI calibration loop.

Solver Benchmarking

Run your data through multiple solver portfolios (GAP-TV, PnP-HQS, FISTA-TV, etc.) and identify the best solver + hyperparameters.

Quality Report

Detailed report with PSNR, SSIM, runtime, parameter sensitivity analysis, and before/after comparisons.

Reproducible RunBundle

All results packaged as a RunBundle with full provenance: git hash, seeds, platform info, SHA256 artifact hashes.

Recommendations

Actionable next steps: measurement protocol improvements, suggested hardware changes, advanced solver configurations.

Follow-Up Support

30-minute debrief call to walk through results and answer questions about integrating calibrated parameters into your pipeline.

How It Works

  1. Intake: Fill out the intake form with your system description and upload measurement data.
  2. Assessment: We review your data and confirm scope, timeline, and pricing within 1 business day.
  3. Calibration: Our team runs the calibration pipeline, benchmarks solvers, and tunes parameters (3-4 business days).
  4. Delivery: You receive the RunBundle, quality report, and recommendations. We schedule a debrief call.
